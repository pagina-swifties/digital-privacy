You are being watched.

Definition
Protection of an individual's information used or created using the internet defines digital privacy. Privacy incorporates two crucial elements-- our state of being alone and not being watched or interrupted by others.
To have digital privacy and confidentiality of all information and data should be protected and be the core values of all tech companies.
Digital privacy also covers the protection and expectation of having your behavior and location not tracked and that using our digital devices can leave digital footprints.
